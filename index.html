We Were Promised Robots: A Gentle Introduction to Robotics and STEM
Saturday, 28 October, 2023

About the workshop

This event is an introductory workshop for teaching the basics of robotics to elementary-aged students as part of the Maryland STEM Festival. This event will introduce students to the history of robotics, and how subjects such as mathematics, science, and art influence how robots look and behave. During this workshop, students will learn fundamental concepts of designing and controlling robots, have hands-on experience with robots from NIST's labs, and will build their own robots from household objects.
Topics that will be discussed:
  • The role of mathematics in the control of robots
  • The impacts of design and materials on robot functionality
  • Logic and the intelligence of robots
  • User interfaces and their role in the user experience

For more information, contact Jeremy Marvel at jeremy.marvel@nist.gov
